Controls:
• Click screen to look (WASD to move, Space to Jump)
• Hold Q to open Spawn Menu & Options
• Left-Click to spawn selected prop
• Right-Click & Hold to Grab & Swing Objects / Players
• Press J: Toggle 1st / 3rd Person View
• Press G: Toggle Fly Mode (Space=Up, Shift=Down)
• Press F: Delete Held Object
• Press Z: Freeze Object Infront of You
